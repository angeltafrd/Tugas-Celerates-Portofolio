PROFILE

EXPERIENCE

UKM Peduli Kemanusiaan

UKM Peduli Kemanusiaan (Humanitarian Care Student Activity Unit) is a student organization that focuses on humanitarian causes. As an active member, I participate in various social and humanitarian initiatives aimed at helping those in need, whether it be through disaster relief efforts, community outreach programs, or fundraising for important causes. This organization allows me to develop my empathy, teamwork, and leadership skills while contributing to meaningful projects that have a positive impact on society.

Pengurus Pondok Pesantren

During my high school years, I was part of the management team at Pondok Pesantren Al-Muhajirin 3 Bahrul Ulum Tambakberas. In this role, I was responsible for helping with the daily operations and activities of the pesantren, fostering a strong sense of community among the students, and contributing to the spiritual and educational growth of my peers. This experience not only enhanced my organizational and leadership abilities but also deepened my understanding of teamwork and responsibility in an Islamic educational environment.

Volunteer WEPOSE Surabaya

I volunteered for WEPOSE Surabaya during their 4th-anniversary celebration, where we distributed 1,000 milk packages to children. The event was themed "Be a Buddy, Not a Bully," aiming to promote kindness and friendship among young children, while raising awareness about the negative impact of bullying. Being part of this initiative allowed me to contribute to a positive cause, encourage empathy, and help foster a supportive environment for the children in our community.
